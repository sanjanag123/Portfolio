Interactive Farm 🐴

What I Learned

Manually coding with p5.js taught me how to blend art and technology creatively. Adding animal sounds made the project more interactive and engaging beyond just visuals on a screen.

Creative Process

I began by designing the grass and sky background, then added the barn, emojis, and text. Integrating the sounds was the hardest part, but seeing it all come together was worth it.

💭 Remember: Animals respond to clicks

🎨 Blue sky + green grass = farm vibes
